Global Biomes Exploration

Embark on a journey through the diverse biomes of our planet, each with its unique ecological tapestry and fascinating biodiversity. Our recent assessment of global biomes highlights the incredible richness and variety of life that exists across the Earth.


Tropical Rainforest Diversity

Delve into the incredible diversity of tropical rainforests, where countless species of plants and animals coexist in a complex web of life. The lush canopy, teeming with biodiversity, makes tropical rainforests one of the most important and threatened biomes on Earth.
